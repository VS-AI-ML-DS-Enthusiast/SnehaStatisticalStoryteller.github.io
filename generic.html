About Me

I hold a B.Tech degree specializing in Electronics and Communication Engineering. My journey into data science began during the Covid lockdown when, as a homemaker, I sought to combine my technical background with a new passion. I started my learning path with Coursera, gaining foundational knowledge in data science. I then pursued further education with a certification from ICT Academy Kerala, followed by an intensive professional certificate program in AI and Machine Learning from IIT Kanpur, which I completed in July 2024.
I am proficient in Python and MySQL, utilizing these programming languages to build and analyze complex data models. My expertise in data visualization includes tools like Tableau, Matplotlib and Seaborn, enabling me to create clear and compelling visual representations of data. In the realm of machine learning and AI, I am skilled in using scikit-learn, TensorFlow and Keras to develop and deploy sophisticated predictive models. With my blend of educational background and practical skills, I excel at transforming raw data into insightful stories, driving decision-making through analytical precision and clear visualizations.
